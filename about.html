My name is Brian Herbert.

From creating “Hello World!” in the console of my first application, to completing college-based assignments and projects, I knew I enjoyed learning about web design and application development. Going back to school was never about getting “just a job”. Instead, this new education has offered an engaging challenge to continually learn and improve upon my skills in logic and development. The simple “Hello World” has formed a new passion that I can get excited by as I continue to strive to become a web developer.
